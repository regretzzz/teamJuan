Unemployment

Unemployment has been a prevalent problem here in the Philippines. Our aim is to lessen the scale of unemployment in the country by helping blue collar people find jobs that they can apply for.

Education

We try and help our users learn various skills needed to meet the minimum job requirements. We do this simply by referring them to links that improve the skill that they desire.

Poverty

By helping solve the unemployment here in our country, we are also helping it rise from poverty.
